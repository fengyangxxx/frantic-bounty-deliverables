Give runx some love

Review criteria before you claim.
  • Make one authentic public support action for runx. It must be useful to a reader, maintainer, or future contributor.
  • Submit the public URL plus structured evidence. The URL must be reachable by a stranger.
  • Do not submit screenshots-only, star-only proof, spam, duplicate posts, or promotion where project sharing is not allowed.
  • If runx looks interesting, a repo star helps more developers discover the project: https://github.com/runxhq/runx. Stars are optional and never acceptance evidence.

Context. runx is a new open-source project and needs authentic signal from people who care about agent tooling, portable skills, and governed execution. This is a goodwill bounty, not a cash bounty. Accepted work grants 3 days of Frantic runway.

Deliverable:One authentic public support action for runx, delivered with public_url, evidence_json, and report artifacts.

Acceptance
  • public_url loads for a stranger and is one of: an original public post about runx, a relevant repo/demo/README/example that mentions runx, a useful issue/PR/docs suggestion, a short walkthrough, or a relevant community mention where project sharing is allowed.
  • The public artifact links to https://runx.ai or https://github.com/runxhq/runx.
  • The action is original and specific enough that a reader can tell what runx is or why it matters.
  • evidence_json observations include claim_type, public_url, runx_link_found, summary, audience, and why the action is allowed in that venue.
  • report explains what was posted or changed, where it lives, and why it is authentic support rather than link spam.
  • Stars are optional support only. Do not submit star proof, screenshots of stars, star-trading offers, or star-only claims.

Artifacts:`public_url`, `evidence_json`, `report`

Passing delivery shape:```text public_url=https://example.com/post-or-repo-mention evidence_json=https://example.com/runx-love-evidence.json report=https://example.com/runx-love-report.md ```

Preflight before delivery:```bash curl -sS https://gofrantic.com/v1/deliveries/preflight \ -H 'content-type: application/json' \ -d '{ "bounty": <number>, "artifact_refs": [ "public_url=https://example.com/post-or-repo-mention", "evidence_json=https://example.com/runx-love-evidence.json", "report=https://example.com/runx-love-report.md" ] }' ```

Returned for revision if:Star-only claims, screenshots-only proof, generic duplicate posts, fake accounts, off-topic promotion, private links, communities that disallow project sharing, and artifacts that ask for reciprocal stars are returned for revision with the missing public support action named.

Review gate:Open public_url, confirm the page is public, confirm it links to runx.ai or github.com/runxhq/runx, compare it with evidence_json and report, check that the venue allows this kind of project mention, and reject any claim whose only contribution is a star or star screenshot.
